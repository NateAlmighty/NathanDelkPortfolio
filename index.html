About Me

I'm Nathan Delk, a certified web accessibility tester with experience conducting manual audits using WAVE, Axe, and NVDA. As someone who uses a screen reader daily due to ADHD, I bring a unique perspective to identifying real-world barriers.

Audit: Accessibility of AccessiBe.com Landing Page

Tools Used: WAVE, Axe, NVDA

WCAG Issues Found: Missing alt text, form label issues, low contrast

Summary: Identified several barriers including unlabeled buttons, missing alt attributes, and poor color contrast affecting readability.
